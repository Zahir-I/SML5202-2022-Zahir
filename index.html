On this website you will be able to learn the fundamentals of Arabic and Bangla

Learn the alphabet, how to count, and ask/respond to basic questions. Use the different interactive elements to get an initial grasp of the languages.


Why Arabic and Bangla?

  • Arabic is the 6th most spoken language in the world
  • Bengali is the 7th most spoken language in the world
  • They are the two most spoken languages in the Muslim world
  • They are both foreign scripts, making them more interesting to learn
